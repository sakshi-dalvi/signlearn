How to practise

Get the most from your session

1

Study first

Visit the Learn page to study the gesture images for each sign before practising.

2

Select a sign

Click a reference card to highlight which sign you're practising.

3

Start camera

4

Compare & refine

Match your hand to the reference image. Start slowly, then build speed.

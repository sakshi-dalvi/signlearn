Why it matters

Language is a human right

Millions of people communicate primarily through sign language every day. Yet for most of the hearing world, it remains unfamiliar — creating daily barriers in education, healthcare, and public life.

Sign language is a rich, complete visual language with its own grammar and structure. Learning even the basics changes how you engage with the world.
